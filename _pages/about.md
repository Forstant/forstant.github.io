## Research and Industry Experience

**Graduate Researcher, Sun Yat-sen University**  
*Sept. 2024 – Mar. 2025, Guangzhou, China*

- Built a two-stage medical LLM on 100K+ clinical records with data standardization, continued pre-training, and multi-task supervised fine-tuning.
- Structured full clinical trajectories, designed six parallel tasks, and applied LoRA with task/data ablations.
- Added physician-annotated chain-of-thought and bootstrap evaluation, achieving around 20% improvement from only 10% CoT labels.
- This work led to an accepted paper in **IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (JBHI)**.

**Algorithm Research Intern, NetEase (Alpha Algorithm Strategy Team)**  
*Jul. 2025 – Jan. 2026, Hangzhou/Guangzhou, China*

- Studied diffusion-based recommendation under dynamic retrieval settings.
- Proposed **DR-Diff**, a process-aware retrieval diffusion framework for sequential recommendation.
- Designed an interval retrieval strategy to reduce latency overhead introduced by dynamic retrieval.
- Achieved better HR@20 and NDCG@20 than strong baselines on three real-world datasets.
- This work is currently under review at **IJCAI 2026**.
